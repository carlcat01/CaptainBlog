The Cantebury is Destroyed

Posted on: 27/04/2346

The ice hauler, the Canterbury has destroyed. After logging the distress call myself and four others took the shuttle to investigate. We discovered a heavily damaged ship, cause unknown. Soon after we entered, the Cant detected another heat signature nearby that did not match any known vessels. We immediately returned to the shuttle and started back. The unknown vessel fired two missiles that came near us, and continued to the Cant. The unknown ship then went dark, and masked it’s heat emissions. I am now discussing with the four survivors what we should do.

The Donnager is Destroyed

Posted on: 08/05/2346

A lot has happened after my last log. We were taken aboard the Donnager and held in the brig. They then proceeded to interview each one of us independently. When they interrogated me, they suggested that Niomi Nagata, our chief engineer, was part of an OPA terrorist cell. I do not believe that. It also tuns out that our pilot, Alex Kamal, is an honorably discharged MCRN pilot. After the interrogations, we were but in a holding cell. I was able to talk to the captain, but during our discussion the unknown vessels attacked. I was able to escape with three of my original crew. Dr. Shed was killed during the attack. The MCRN LT and marine that got us to this new ship, the Tachi, have died. The LT transferred control of the Tachi to me. We barley left the hangar before the entire flagship of the MCRN, the Donnager, was destroyed. After the attack, I now worry that it is not the Martians’, but some unknown party.

A New Mission

Posted on: 15/05/2346

The meeting with Fred was uneasy at first. We eventually were able to make an agreement that he will support my testimony that it was not the Martians that destroyed the Canterbury. In return I had to have to find a survivor of one his OPA ships, the Scapuli. I have also informed him all that I know about the destruction of the Canterbury and Donnager. He shares my thoughts that both attacks were performed by an unknown party. The Rocinante has been fitted to appear as a gas freighter, and will be cred the three survivors and myself. The last known location of the survivor we were looking for is on Eros station. We will be departing shortly.
